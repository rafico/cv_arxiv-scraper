Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need Gmail?

No. Gmail is only for emailing yourself digests. The app works normally without it.

Do I need the AI features?

No. The app matches and ranks papers using your authors, labs, and topics. AI summaries are optional polish.

Why is my inbox empty?

Your tracked terms are very narrow, or there were no matches in the selected timeframe. Try broader topics or a wider time window.

What should I learn first?

Three things: add interests, run a scrape, and review papers with Save or Not Interested. Everything else can wait.

Does the search bar search all of arXiv?

No. The dashboard search bar searches papers already in your library. Use Run Scrape for new feed papers or Discover for older arXiv papers.

What’s the difference between keyword and semantic search?

Keyword finds exact words. Semantic finds papers by meaning. Hybrid combines both.

What are collections vs. tags?

Collections are named folders — good for grouping related papers. Tags are free-form labels — good for cross-cutting categories. A paper can have both.

Can I monitor categories besides cs.CV?

Yes. Add extra feed sources in Settings or via the API.

How do I backfill old papers?

Use the Discover page, or run cv-arxiv-sync --category cs.CV --from 2024-01-01 --to 2024-03-31 from the CLI.

How do I export my papers?

BibTeX (single or bulk), HTML report, or sync to Mendeley/Zotero. See the Export & Sync page.
