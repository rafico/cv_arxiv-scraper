How Ranking Works

You do not need to understand the scoring formula to use the app well. If something looks out of place, use Save or Not Interested and the ranking will adjust over time.

In plain English, papers usually rise to the top when:

  • They match more of the authors, labs, or topics you entered.
  • They are newer.
  • You have previously saved similar papers.
  • AI scoring is enabled and the paper looks relevant.

Enrichment & Embeddings

OpenAlex

Papers are automatically enriched with OpenAlex data during scrapes: topic classifications, open-access status, and citation counts. Configure in config.yaml:

openalex:
  enabled: true        # set to false to disable
  email: ""            # your email for the polite pool (higher rate limits)

SPECTER2 Embeddings

SPECTER2 paper embeddings are generated automatically during scrapes and stored in a FAISS index. These power semantic search and related-paper recommendations. The model (~420MB) is downloaded on first use and cached in ~/.cache/huggingface/. No configuration needed.
