Quick Start

Note: Start simple

If you are new, focus on the basics first. You can ignore Gmail and AI until the core app is working for you.

1

Add interests

Add authors, labs, or topics in Settings → Research Setup.

2

Run Scrape

Click the Run Scrape button in the top navigation to collect matching papers.

3

Review Inbox

On the Papers page, use Save for good papers and Not Interested for bad matches.

Your Normal Workflow

Step 1

Tell it what you care about

In Research Setup, enter one item per line. Start small: a few authors, a few labs, and a few topic phrases.

Step 2

Run a scrape

Press Run Scrape. The app will fetch recent papers and build your inbox with matches.

Tip: You only need to scrape once a day. ArXiv typically updates its feed at 8pm EST.

Step 3

Review the inbox

Open the Papers page. Save the papers you want to keep, and mark weak matches as Not Interested.

Step 4

Come back to Saved

The Saved tab is your shortlist. Use it for papers you want to revisit, cite, or share.
